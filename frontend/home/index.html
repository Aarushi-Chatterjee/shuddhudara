About Us

Vidaverde International

Vidaverde International is a globally recognized organization dedicated to environmental sustainability, ecological restoration, and green innovation. Founded with a mission to create climate-positive strategies that empower communities, Vidaverde focuses on promoting natural, organic, and botanical solutions to environmental challenges. The organization works on large-scale ecosystem restoration projects and advocates for living naturally while reducing the environmental impact of daily activities. Through innovative programs and partnerships, Vidaverde International contributes significantly to caring for our planet and fostering sustainable development worldwide.

YDfCT: Youth Development for Climate Tech

The Youth Development for Climate Tech (YDfCT) program, supported by Meta AI, represents a transformative cross-border innovation initiative that empowers young climate innovators across Singapore, India, and Indonesia. This program equips students with cutting-edge AI and data tools to address critical real-world climate challenges including renewable energy, air pollution, extreme heat, and flooding. YDfCT guides participants through the complete innovation journeyâ€”from ideation and prototype development with professional mentorship to solution deployment. Top innovations receive funding opportunities, enabling young leaders to transform their climate technology ideas into scalable solutions that create meaningful environmental impact in their communities and beyond.
