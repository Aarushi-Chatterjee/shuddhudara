About Us

Vidaverde International

A globally recognized organization dedicated to environmental sustainability, ecological restoration, and green innovation. Vidaverde focuses on promoting natural, organic, and botanical solutions to environmental challenges, working on large-scale ecosystem restoration projects and advocating for sustainable living.

YDfCT: Youth Development for Climate Tech

Supported by Meta AI, this transformative cross-border innovation initiative empowers young climate innovators across Singapore, India, and Indonesia. The program equips students with AI and data tools to address critical climate challenges like renewable energy, air pollution, and extreme heat.
